Vijona Africa

We love To Impact!

Africa has the largest youth population in the world with exponential growth projections by 2050. This “youth bubble” could be an asset or a liability for the developing continent.


Relindis Mbu


Relindis Mbu IT Consultant, with 16 years in the Corporate Banking and Health Insurance industries. Leverages “best of both worlds” vantage point (21 years in Africa; born and raised and 21 years in North America), cultural context and global worldview to engineer solutions for the African market. Founder of Vijona Africa, a US based nonprofit organization focused on capacity building for younger Africans (7-17 year olds), igniting critical thinking and innate talent, prepping them to stay relevant and competitive in their generation. Loves the outdoors, a marathon runner and climber of Africa’s highest peak; Mount Kilimanjaro.

Kathy Knox


Kathy Knox is a Certified Behavior Strategist. She’s passionate about what she calls the 3-P’s: People, Personality, & Purpose! In addition to helping young people discover their unique strengths, Kathy’s training also reveals how to unite these strengths collectively to maximize the power of community. Kathy has a background in facilitation and education, as well as graduate studies in Industrial/Organizational Psychology. She is married with three college-age girls, and currently based in Southern California.

Gina


Regina Dettmer is a writer and activist based in Oakland, California. She served as a Peace Corps Volunteer in Cameroon from 2015-2017 and has been collaborating on educational projects in West Africa ever since.
